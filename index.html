About Me

I'm Jona, a second year computer science student at the University of California, Irvine. I like programming, math, music, and anything tech. The summer after high school, I got into game development with Unity. I studied C# from Jamie King's C# tutorials on YouTube as the same time I picked up Unity, and I used those skills to prototype a dungeon crawler the summer after that. It was an amazing experience!

I also recently developed an interest in music composition and sound design. I am currently working on developing a virtual reality DAW in a research project.

Finally, I recently became interested in web development. I am amazed at the power of websites because they have the power to influence many people at once. With just a spark of inspiration, someone with web development skills could provide a vaulable service to anyone browsing the web.

My experience at UC Irvine has been great so far. I'm looking forward to learning a lot about current technologies and applying my knowledge to doing something awesome. In the meantime, I'll continue building this website and making it look more elegant.
